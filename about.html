    Our Story

    How we started and where we're going

    2020 - The Beginning

    Founded with a simple idea: make event ticketing easier for everyone.

    2021 - First Milestone

    Launched our platform and hosted our first 100 events.

    2022 - Rapid Growth

    Expanded to 50 cities and reached 10,000 events hosted.

    2023 - Innovation

    Introduced AI-powered event recommendations and mobile app.

    2024 - Global Expansion

    Now serving millions of users across 100+ countries worldwide.
