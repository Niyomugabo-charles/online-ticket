    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about EventTicketing

    Getting Started

    Creating an account is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your details including name, email, and password, and verify your email address. Once verified, you can start exploring events and purchasing tickets.
    Yes! EventTicketing is free for users to browse events and create accounts. We only charge a small service fee on ticket purchases, which is clearly displayed during checkout. Event organizers have different pricing tiers based on their needs.
    To become an event organizer, you need to submit an application through your account dashboard. We'll review your application and typically approve it within 1-2 business days. Once approved, you can start creating and managing events.

    Tickets & Purchases

    Browse events on our platform, select the event you want to attend, choose your ticket type and quantity, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in, provide payment information, and confirm your purchase. Tickets will be sent to your email.
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our trusted payment partners.
    Refund policies vary by event and are set by the event organizer. Check the event details page for specific refund information. Some events offer refunds up to a certain date, while others may be non-refundable. Service fees are always non-refundable.
    After purchase, you'll receive an email confirmation with your tickets. You can also access your tickets anytime through your account dashboard under "My Tickets." Tickets are available as both digital QR codes and printable PDFs.

    Events

    You can find events by browsing our events page, using the search bar to look for specific events or artists, filtering by category, date, location, or price range. We also provide personalized recommendations based on your interests and past purchases.
    Once you're approved as an organizer, go to your dashboard and click "Create Event." Fill in event details including title, description, date, time, location, ticket types, and prices. Your event will be reviewed and typically published within 24 hours.
    Yes! We offer various promotional tools including featured listings, social media promotion, email marketing to interested users, and analytics to track your event's performance. Contact our support team for custom promotion packages.

    Account & Security

    Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the link in the email to create a new password. The link expires after 24 hours for security reasons.
    Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. All payment data is processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment processors. We never store credit card information on our servers.
    You can delete your account from your account settings. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Any active tickets or pending transactions will be canceled. We recommend downloading your data before deleting your account.

    Technical Issues

    First, check that you're logged into the correct account. If you still can't see your tickets, check your email for the purchase confirmation. If the issue persists, contact our support team with your order number and we'll help resolve it.
    Check that your payment information is correct and that you have sufficient funds. If the issue persists, try a different payment method or contact your bank. Sometimes payments fail due to security blocks - you may need to contact your bank to authorize the transaction.
    Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, ensure you have a stable internet connection, and try using a different browser. If the problem persists, check our status page for any known issues or contact our technical support team.
