    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I create an account?
    Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation, fill in your details including name, email, and password, then click "Create Account". You'll receive a verification email - click the link in that email to activate your account.
    I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
    Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires in 30 minutes for security reasons.
    How do I become an event organizer?
    After creating an account, you can apply to become an organizer by filling out the organizer application form. Go to your dashboard and look for the "Become an Organizer" option. Our team will review your application within 3-5 business days.
    How do I buy tickets for an event?
    Browse events on our platform, click on the event you're interested in, select your preferred ticket category and quantity, then click "Reserve Tickets". You'll have 15 minutes to complete the purchase before the reservation expires.
    What is a ticket reservation?
    A reservation holds tickets for you for 15 minutes while you complete the checkout process. This prevents others from buying the same tickets while you're making your payment decision.
    How do I access my e-tickets?
    After purchasing tickets, you can access them from your dashboard. Each ticket has a unique QR code that you can show at the venue entrance. You can also download your tickets as PDF files.
    How do I search for events?
    Use the search bar on the homepage or events page to search by event name, artist, or venue. You can also filter events by category, price range, date, and location using the filter options.
    Can I get a refund for my tickets?
    Refund policies vary by event organizer. Check the event details page for the specific refund policy. Some events offer full refunds up to a certain date, while others may have no refund policy.
    What payment methods do you accept?
    We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
    Is my payment information secure?
    Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your credit card details on our servers - all payment processing is handled by secure third-party payment processors.
    Why isn't my QR code scanning?
    Make sure your phone screen brightness is turned up and the QR code is clearly visible. If it still doesn't work, you can show your order confirmation email or the ticket details from your dashboard as an alternative.
    The website is loading slowly. What can I do?
    Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try using a different browser. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If problems persist, our technical team may be performing maintenance.
